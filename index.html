About Me

I am a Georgia native, originally from Augusta. My hobbies include riding my mountain bike on trails, reading books (currently going through the Dune series), and spoiling my two cats. I am currently setting my sights on living within the Atlanta area and starting a career in web development.

My field of work has varied the last ten years, from teacher, librarian, bartender, and restaurant manager. Despite my knack working within the service industry and alongside others, one of my favorite pastimes is working with computers and learning about technology.

My academic background includes a Bacherlor of Arts in English Composition, with side studies in Computer Science, and a Master of Arts in Literature. What I initially lack in a formal Computer Science degree, I make up it with ingenuity, tenacity, and an eagerness to learn.
